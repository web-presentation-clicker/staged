On-Stage

No presentation clicker?...No problem!

On-Stage is a simple app that allows you to remotely connect
and control your Google Slide presentation to your mobile device.

Download Now

Step-By-Step Instructions:

  1. Download the extension! (Click on "Download Now")
  2. Add the extension the to your browser (See browser notes)
  3. Open Google Slides
  4. Select the extension to generate a QR Code
  5. Scan the QR code with your mobile device to pair with Google Slides
  6. In Google Slides, select the "Slideshow" button or press Ctrl+F5
  7. Once the slideshow begins, your mobile device will allow you to move to the next or previous slides

Browser Notes:

Here's a summary of how to manage our extension with your respective browser:

  • Open your browser and enable Developer mode
  • Select the extension downloaded extension to be added to your extensions

For Safari:

  • Open the extension as an Xcode project and export it as an .app
  • Open Safari, go to Preferences then Extensions
  • Enable the extension
